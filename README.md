# LOGOMARK
This project is a personal branding assignment that focuses on creating a simple logomark and infographic that represent my identity, goals, and future career in technology.  The assignment combines design thinking and communication to visually present who I am and the career path I am working toward.
The project includes a simple black and white logomark built from the letters M and G, representing my name Mduduzi Gama. Design requirements. Simple enough to redraw by hand Works in black and white Uses minimal shapes Represents my personal identity. The infographic grows from the logomark and visually communicates my career journey.

The infographic includes:

My name and personal identity

My current education (BSc IT)

My goal of becoming a software engineer

My commitment to building a successful career in technology
